The website contains a progress
tracker is meant to help artists that
strive to become more involved with
the local music scene.

The tasks provided will help the
users to gain an understanding of
the local music scene, to make
connections, and to grow as artists.

You need to sign up so you can start
tracking your progress!

Many artists have their own
journeys in the local music scene,
and their stories are personal and
unique to themselves. Many
established artists had gone
through their own trials and
troubles before making it to where
they are today.

These are stories of various artists
about their own experiences and
insights over the course of their
own journeys.
